Provider Accounts: Overview

This section covers provider account management tasks.

Currently, Nimbul supports Amazon EC2 only.

Cluster Administrators N/A

Cluster administrators do not have access to provider accounts. Note, however, that when a user adds a provider account to the system, he or she effectively becomes a provider account administrator with full access to the new account. For more information, see Adding a Provider Account.
